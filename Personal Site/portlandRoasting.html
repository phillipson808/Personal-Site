Portland Roasting Coffee


Problem: Portland Roasting Coffee does a lot more than brew delicious beverages, they give back to the communities that farm their ingredients. The brand has the potential to tell a story that would allow them to stand out from the competition while creating a loyal bond with their consumers. Unfortunately, that story is being overlooked.


Solution: Spread awareness about Portland Roasting Coffee's global projects while giving the brand a fresh image to attract younger consumers.
