Timberland

Ad Campaign

Timberland


Problem: Timberland's fight for sustainability has been pushing for a brighter future through various avenues. However, many of their consumers are unaware of their efforts to reduce environmental harm.


Solution: Create a limited edition line of shoes that promotes their sustainable efforts while also creating a way for consumers to contribute to the cause. Each pair of shoes will come with seeds for a plant indigenous to the buyers area. This will allow the consumer to feel included in Timberland's sustainable movement while building hype for the brand.
