Puffs

Ad campaign

Puffs


Problem: Kleenex is such a dominant facial tissue product that many people believe facial tissue is actually called Kleenex. 55 million people have used Puffs in the past year compared to 167 million people that have used Kleenex.


Solution: By using the world of sports in a unique way, we leveraged the idea of crying when your team loses as a fun way to spread Puffs as a brand. Let's face it, facial tissue is a pretty boring product, let's give people a fun reason to purchase it!
